Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2026

Simple Exercise Tracker ("the App") is developed by Scott Yost. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

Simple Exercise Tracker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All workout data you enter is stored locally on your device only.

Data Storage

Your workout history, exercise logs, and preferences are saved on your device using local storage. This data is not accessible to us or any third party.

Analytics and Tracking

The App does not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services and does not share your data with anyone.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by deleting individual workouts within the App or by uninstalling the App from your device. Uninstalling the App permanently removes all stored data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children. The App stores no personal information whatsoever.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact the developer at the support email listed on the App Store.
